About DracoinLabs Nexus

We are seeking an experienced Senior Solana Blockchain Engineer to help review, architect, and enhance DracoinLabs Nexus — a Solana-based Web3 platform on DracoinLabs.

DracoinLabs Nexus currently includes core token functionality and wallet integration. The next phase of development will evolve the platform into a comprehensive Web3 ecosystem featuring staking, governance, treasury management, analytics, and community-driven functionality.

This role is ideal for an engineer who enjoys both hands-on development and high-level architectural decision-making on production Solana infrastructure.

Responsibilities

Architecture & technical leadership

  • Review the existing DracoinLabs Nexus Solana codebase and architecture
  • Evaluate smart contract design and security
  • Identify scalability and performance improvements
  • Define technical standards and best practices
  • Create a development roadmap and implementation strategy

Blockchain development

  • Design and develop Solana programs using Rust and Anchor Framework
  • Implement staking and rewards mechanisms
  • Design governance and voting systems
  • Build treasury management functionality
  • Integrate wallet connectivity and transaction workflows
  • Improve smart contract security and testing coverage

Collaboration

  • Work closely with product and engineering stakeholders
  • Participate in technical planning and architecture discussions
  • Produce technical documentation and implementation guidelines
  • Support deployment and maintenance activities
